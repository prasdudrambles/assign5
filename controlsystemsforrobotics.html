Control System for Robotics

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of control systems and their applications in robotics engineering.

Course Overview:

  • Introduction to Control Systems
  • Feedback Control and PID
  • State-Space Representation
  • Robotics Control and Actuators

Course Objectives:

  • Master control system principles.
  • Design and implement control algorithms for robotics.
  • Analyze and optimize control performance.
  • Integrate control systems into robotic applications.
