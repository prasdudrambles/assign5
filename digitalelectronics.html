Introduction to Digital Electronics

This course provides a strong foundation in digital systems and logic design, essential for electronics and robotics engineering.

Course Overview:

  • Number Systems and Binary Arithmetic
  • Logic Gates and Boolean Algebra
  • Combinational and Sequential Circuits
  • Digital Logic Design

Course Objectives:

  • Understand digital electronics principles.
  • Design and analyze digital circuits.
  • Develop problem-solving abilities.
  • Prepare for advanced engineering courses.
